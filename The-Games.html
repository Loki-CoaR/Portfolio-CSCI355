The Games

November 2025 - February 2026

Overview

The game revolves around the protagonist house in a snow storm that has rocked the east coast and guests who need shelter from the storm. Guests can be good or bad, with unclear intentions and even shadier motives.

The Rules of the Game.

At night, every night, the player must choose from a group of guests to let into their house and can check the Windows to observe the surroundings. The list updates every night as more people enter. There is a variety of items that can influence how your DAY time goes: some can restore the sanity of the house, while others deplete it, letting the player end the day early and go to bed if they prefer.

Endings

The Game contains several endings that are largely dependent on player choice.

Information Tab

About the Endings...
  • Bad Endings: Bad endings result in certain doom for the player or a very uncertain future.
  • Ambiguous Endings: Ambiguous endings are endings in which the player's fate is not clearly given in the ending.
  • Good Endings: Good endings are achieved by making the right choices and navigating the game's challenges successfully.

More about the GAMES!!!

Simplified RPG Loop
  • Dynamic Narrative Delivery: Acts as a mini simplified RPG of the universe, offering a highly stylized alternative to heavy lore dumping by introducing characters organically.
  • The Day Cycle: Players are limited to selecting exactly 3 NPCs to interact with per day cycle, driving targeted narrative exploration.
  • Resource Management: Features a strict 3-heart health system alongside survival maintenance mechanics, including critical food and sustenance requirements.
